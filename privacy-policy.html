PRIVACY POLICY

1. Introduction

The terms and conditions of this privacy policy (hereinafter the “Privacy Policy”) govern the use of all Yotako Services. By using Yotako Services you agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. We may modify or update this Privacy Policy from time to time, so please review it periodically. Your continued use of Yotako services after any modification of this Privacy Policy will constitute your acceptance of such modification. This Privacy Policy was updated on June 13, 2017.

2. Information we collect

To use Yotako services you need to create an account and provide us with personal information such as email address, user name, password, etc.

We also collect information from other sources when you sign in using third parties services (Google, Github). This usually includes your public information on these third parties services and your email address.

We may also collect some information automatically when you use our services: the IP address and the characteristics of your device, your browsing history and the actions made on our services, etc. Such information may be either collected directly by Yotako or through the use of third parties analytics services. In order to collect such information, we may use cookies and similar technologies. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer when using our services. These text files can be read and help to identify you when you return to our services. You can control how we use cookies on your device by modifying your browser’s cookie settings. Please note that if you set your browser to disable cookies or other technologies, you may not be able to access certain parts of our services and other parts of our services may not work properly.

3. How we use your information

The information that we collect may be used notably for the following purposes:

  • Used to identify you when you log in on our services.
  • To operate your Yotako account and to give you access to our services.
  • To send you updates, newsletters and email notifications about our services.
  • To respond to you about any comment or enquiry you have submitted.
  • To customize your use of our services and to improve the quality of our services.
  • Used for market research purposes
  • Used for billing purpose

By accepting this Privacy Policy, you expressly accept that Yotako uses your personal information for the purposes above.

4. Transfer of your information

Your personal information will not be shared with third parties, except as described below: Yotako may resort to subcontractors that are located outside the territory of Luxembourg for services optimization purposes. In this context, Yotako may resort to subcontractors located in the European Union and in the United States solely for the purposes of providing Yotako with client relationship management services and marketing and emailing services. If required by law we may disclose personal information to competent authorities.

5. Your choices about your information

You can unsubscribe at any time from email updates and newsletter by clicking on the “unsubscribe” link at the bottoms of said updates and newsletters.

Any information you provided to Yotako when you registered an account may be accessed, modified or deleted at any time by contacting legal@yotako.io. Please note that deleting mandatory information may prevent you from using the related services.

Concerning information not available in your account settings you can also request us to provide you with a list of all of your personal information stored by Yotako by sending an email in this regard to the following email address:legal@yotako.io.
